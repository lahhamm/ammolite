Privacy

Last updated July 2026

This is a plain-language summary of how we handle information on this site, written to be actually readable rather than to bury the point. It's not a substitute for formal legal advice, but it is an honest account of what happens.

What we collect

The only information this site collects is what you choose to submit through the contact form: your name, email, business name (optional), and your message. We don't use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies anywhere on this site.

What we do with it

Your message is sent directly to our inbox so we can reply to your inquiry. That's it. We don't sell it, rent it, or use it for marketing lists. It's read by a human, not processed by anything automated beyond delivering the email.

Who else sees it

We use Resend, an email delivery service, purely as the pipe that carries your message from this form to our inbox. They process it only to deliver that email and are bound by their own privacy terms.

How long we keep it

We keep inquiry messages only as long as reasonably needed to respond and follow up. If you'd like your information deleted sooner, just ask.

Questions

Reach out any time through the contact form.
